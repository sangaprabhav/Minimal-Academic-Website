Research

My research focuses on multi-modal AI and more recently on machine unlearning with applications to medical image analysis. My recent works explore:

  • Post training class and style forgetting (MUGen ICML'25, U&Me ICCV'25)
  • Knowledge graph enhanced dermatological diagnosis (Master's Thesis)
  • Test-time adaptation for inference-time robustness (ViSCALE CVPR'25)
